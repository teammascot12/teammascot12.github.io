Daredevil - Lawyer By Day, Superhero By Night

This page is specifically about the Marvel Superhero known as Daredevil. His given name is Matt Murdock. Matt Murdock is a man who was blinded by a radioactive substance that while blinding him, gave him supernatural heightened senses. Some of his super attributes include super hearing, heat sensitivity and sonar-like detection of objects around him. Once he discovered his abilities, he realized he could use these powers for good by fighting crime.

In time, he learned that fighting crime is not easy and requires more than just super senses. So, he studied matial arts and learned fighting skills to go along with his new super abilities. Oh ... and he's a lawyer. So there's that.
