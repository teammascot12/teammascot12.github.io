Moon Knight - A Crazy Dude Who Fights Crime

This page is specifically about the Marvel Superhero known as Moon Knight. His real name is Marc Spector, although he's crazy, so sometimes he thinks he's a few other people too. He gets his power from Khonshu, a sort of Egyptian god who is also sometimes his enemy ... sort of. It's complicated. Afterall, he is crazy.
